## About WorldHeritage.guide

WorldHeritage.guide is your comprehensive, independent travel guide to Germany's rich cultural and natural heritage. We provide detailed, factually accurate information about UNESCO World Heritage Sites, historic monuments, architectural treasures, and natural wonders across Germany.

### Our Mission

To make Germany's extraordinary heritage accessible to travelers worldwide through high-quality, detailed guides that inspire and inform.

### What We Offer

- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Detailed guides to 1000+ heritage sites across Germany
- **UNESCO Focus**: Complete coverage of all 51 UNESCO World Heritage Sites
- **Accurate Information**: All data sourced from Wikidata and verified sources
- **Travel Planning**: Practical visiting information, tips, and recommendations
- **Rich Media**: High-quality images from Wikimedia Commons
- **Interactive Maps**: OpenStreetMap integration for every site

### Our Approach

We believe in:
- **Factual Accuracy**: Every detail is sourced from reliable databases
- **Educational Value**: Deep historical and cultural context
- **Practical Usefulness**: Real visiting information for real travelers
- **Open Data**: Using and attributing open-source data

### Data Sources

This website uses data from:
- **Wikidata**: Structured heritage site information
- **Wikimedia Commons**: High-quality heritage images
- **OpenStreetMap**: Interactive mapping

### Independent & Educational

**Important**: WorldHeritage.guide is an independent travel guide platform. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to UNESCO or the World Heritage Centre. "UNESCO World Heritage Site" is a registered trademark of UNESCO. We use this term descriptively for educational purposes.
