## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 11, 2026

### Overview

WorldHeritage.guide ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

### Information We Collect

**Analytics Data**: We use analytics services to understand site usage:
- Page views and navigation patterns
- Device type and browser information
- Geographic location (country/city level)
- Referral sources

**No Personal Data**: We do NOT collect:
- Names or email addresses (unless you contact us)
- Payment information (we don't sell anything)
- Login credentials (no user accounts)

### How We Use Information

We use analytics data to:
- Improve site content and user experience
- Understand which heritage sites are most popular
- Optimize site performance
- Plan future content

### Third-Party Services

We use:
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics / Cloudflare Analytics
- **Hosting**: Cloudflare Pages
- **Maps**: OpenStreetMap (privacy-friendly, no tracking)

### Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:
- Analytics (anonymous usage tracking)
- Site functionality

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

### Data Storage

- Analytics data is stored by third-party providers
- No personal data is stored on our servers
- Contact form submissions (if added) will be processed securely

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (if any is collected)
- Request deletion
- Opt-out of analytics (use browser settings)

### Children's Privacy

This site is educational and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
