## Overview

The Graphic Art Collection of the City of Nuremberg is a distinguished art museum dedicated to the preservation and exhibition of graphic arts. Located in the heart of Nuremberg, Germany, this collection serves as a crucial cultural institution, showcasing a diverse range of prints and illustrations that highlight the evolution of graphic art from various historical periods. The museum is part of the broader Kunstsammlungen der Stadt Nürnberg, which enriches the cultural landscape of the city.

Nuremberg, with its deep-rooted history and artistic heritage, provides an ideal backdrop for this collection.
