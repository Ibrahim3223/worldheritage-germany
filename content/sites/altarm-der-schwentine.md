The reserve serves not only as a sanctuary for nature lovers but also as an essential ecological site that highlights the importance of preserving natural landscapes. Visitors to Altarm der Schwentine can expect a serene environment, perfect for leisurely exploration and reflection amidst the beauty of nature.

As a nature reserve, it plays a crucial role in conservation efforts, emphasizing the importance of protecting such areas for future generations. 

Whether you are a local resident or a traveler seeking solace in nature, the Altarm der Schwentine promises a refreshing escape into the heart of Schleswig-Holstein's natural world.
