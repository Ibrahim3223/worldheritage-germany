## Nearby Attractions

If you're planning to explore beyond the Museum of Art and Crafts Hamburg, several nearby attractions are worth your time:

1. **Hamburg Kunsthalle**: This prominent art museum features an impressive collection of European art from the Middle Ages to contemporary works. Its expansive galleries and rotating exhibitions make it a must-see for art lovers.

2. **Elbphilharmonie**: An architectural marvel, this concert hall is known for its stunning design and acoustics. Visitors can enjoy a concert or simply take in the views from its plaza, which provides panoramic vistas of the city.

3. **Speicherstadt**: A UNESCO World Heritage site, this historic warehouse district offers a glimpse into Hamburg's trading past. The charming red brick buildings and canals create a picturesque setting for a leisurely stroll.

4. **Miniatur Wunderland**: This miniature world is the largest model railway exhibition in the world. It features highly detailed landscapes and scenes, captivating visitors of all ages.

5. **St. Michael's Church**: One of Hamburg's most famous landmarks, this baroque church offers visitors the chance to climb its tower for a spectacular view of the city and the harbor.
