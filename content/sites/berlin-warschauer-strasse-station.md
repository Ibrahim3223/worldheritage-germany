As a site of historical and architectural interest, Warschauer Straße station invites exploration, offering insights into Berlin's past and the transformations it has undergone since its inception.

1. **East Side Gallery**: Just a short walk from Warschauer Straße station, this open-air gallery features murals painted on the remnants of the Berlin Wall. It offers a unique artistic perspective on the historical significance of the wall and its impact on the city.

2. **Oberbaum Bridge**: An iconic landmark of Berlin, this bridge connects the districts of Friedrichshain and Kreuzberg. Its striking design and historical importance make it a popular spot for photos and leisurely strolls.

3. **RAW Gelände**: A cultural space and creative hub, RAW Gelände is home to various bars, clubs, and art studios. This area has transformed from a former railway yard into a vibrant community space, reflecting Berlin's contemporary artistic scene.

5. **Berlin Arena**: A venue for concerts and events, it is located close to the station. Check local listings for upcoming performances and activities to enhance your visit.
