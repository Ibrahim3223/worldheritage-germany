This unique site offers visitors a chance to explore the intricacies of slate production, a pivotal aspect of the region's industrial heritage. With its engaging exhibits and hands-on experiences, Deutsches Schieferbergwerk provides a comprehensive look into a trade that has shaped the landscape and economy of Mayen for centuries.

The Deutsches Schieferbergwerk stands as a crucial point of reference for understanding the evolution of slate mining practices and the industries that have emerged around it.
