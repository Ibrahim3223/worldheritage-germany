This iconic venue has become synonymous with the spirit of Berlin, reflecting both the city's commitment to sports and its dynamic cultural scene. The Velodrom's design and functionality cater to a diverse audience, making it an essential stop on any exploration of Berlin's architectural and sporting heritage.

As you explore the Velodrom, you will encounter the energy of sports culture intertwined with the excitement of live performances, making it a versatile venue that adapts to the needs of its time.
