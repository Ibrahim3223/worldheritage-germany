## Nearby Attractions

The National Gallery is situated in an area rich with cultural landmarks. After your visit, consider exploring these nearby attractions:

1. **Museum Island**: A UNESCO World Heritage site, this cluster of museums includes the Pergamon Museum and the Altes Museum, providing a comprehensive look at art and history from various cultures.
2. **Berlin Cathedral (Berliner Dom)**: Located adjacent to Museum Island, this magnificent cathedral offers stunning architecture and panoramic views from its dome.
3. **Brandenburg Gate**: A short distance away, this iconic 18th-century neoclassical monument is a symbol of Berlin and a must-see for any visitor.
4. **Reichstag Building**: The seat of the German parliament, this historic building features a glass dome that provides visitors with a unique perspective of the city.
5. **Tiergarten**: Berlin's largest park, ideal for a leisurely stroll or a picnic after your museum visit, offering a tranquil escape from the urban bustle.
