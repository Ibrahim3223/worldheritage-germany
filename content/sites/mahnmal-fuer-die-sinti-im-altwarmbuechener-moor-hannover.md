## Overview

The Mahnmal für die Sinti im Altwarmbüchener Moor stands as a poignant memorial located in the Bothfeld-Vahrenheide district of Hannover, Germany. This site is dedicated to the memory of the Sinti, a group that, along with the Roma, suffered immensely during the Holocaust. As a place of reflection and remembrance, it invites visitors to contemplate the tragic history and cultural heritage of the Sinti people. 

Situated within a natural landscape, the memorial serves not only as a tribute to those who lost their lives but also as an educational point that fosters awareness about the injustices faced by marginalized communities. By visiting, individuals can gain insight into the broader context of discrimination and the importance of preserving the memory of those who were persecuted.
