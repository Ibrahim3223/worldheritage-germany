2. **Documentation Center Nazi Party Rally Grounds**: This museum provides insights into Nuremberg's historical significance during the Nazi era. It is an essential stop for those looking to understand the complex history of the city.

3. **St. Lorenz Church**: One of Nuremberg's most important Gothic churches, St. Lorenz is known for its stunning architecture and beautiful stained glass windows. It is located within walking distance from the park.

4. **Germanisches Nationalmuseum**: As the largest museum of cultural history in the German-speaking region, this museum features extensive collections that showcase the cultural heritage of Germany.
