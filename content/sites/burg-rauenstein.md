The remnants of the outer walls suggest a substantial fortification system that once encircled the castle, indicative of the era's military architecture. Visitors can observe the thick stone walls, which still stand in places, offering a glimpse into the castle's formidable presence.

The overall atmosphere of Burg Rauenstein, with its weathered stones and the encroaching nature, invites contemplation and exploration, making it a fascinating site for both history and architecture enthusiasts.
