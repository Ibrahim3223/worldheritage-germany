The Order's influence reached its zenith during the late Middle Ages, as they established a significant presence in various territories, particularly in present-day Germany and Poland.

The museum itself serves as a repository of the Order's extensive history, showcasing artifacts and documents that reveal the complexities of their operations, governance, and cultural contributions.
