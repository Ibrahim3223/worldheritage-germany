## History and Significance

The history of the Jewish community in Osnabrück traces back several centuries, with the synagogue serving as the heart of this vibrant community. In 2010, the synagogue underwent a reopening that further strengthened its role in the community. The synagogue today serves as a gathering place for religious observances, cultural events, and educational programs aimed at promoting understanding of Jewish traditions and values.
