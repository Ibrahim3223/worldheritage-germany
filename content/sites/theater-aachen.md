## Nearby Attractions

Theater Aachen is conveniently located near several notable attractions that complement a cultural visit to the city. 

1. **Aachen Cathedral**: Just a short walk from the theater, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is famous for its stunning architecture and historical significance as the burial site of Charlemagne.
   
2. **Elisenbrunnen**: A beautiful neoclassical building that houses the city's thermal springs, it is a perfect spot to relax and enjoy the surrounding gardens.

3. **The Centre Charlemagne**: This museum focuses on the history of Aachen, offering insights into its role as a historical and cultural center in Europe.

4. **Puppenbrunnen (Puppet Fountain)**: A charming fountain adorned with puppets, this site is a delightful reminder of Aachen's playful artistic spirit.

5. **Couven Museum**: Located nearby, this museum showcases the history of Aachen's domestic life, featuring period furnishings and decorative arts.
