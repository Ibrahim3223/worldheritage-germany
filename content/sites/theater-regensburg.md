## Nearby Attractions

While visiting Theater Regensburg, consider exploring some of the nearby attractions that complement your cultural experience:

1. **Regensburg Cathedral (Dom St. Peter)**: A stunning example of Gothic architecture, this cathedral offers impressive stained glass windows and a chance to explore its historical significance.
   
2. **Old Stone Bridge (Steinerne Brücke)**: An iconic medieval bridge that spans the Danube River, providing lovely views of the city and its surroundings.
   
3. **Walhalla Memorial**: A short trip from the city center, this neoclassical monument honors distinguished figures in German history and offers panoramic views of the Danube Valley.

4. **Thurn und Taxis Palace**: Explore the opulent interiors and gardens of this former noble residence, which hosts various cultural events throughout the year.

5. **Regensburg’s Historic Center**: A UNESCO World Heritage site, the historic center is home to well-preserved medieval buildings, quaint streets, and lively squares, perfect for leisurely exploration.
