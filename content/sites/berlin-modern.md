While visiting Berlin Modern, consider exploring other cultural landmarks nearby. Here are some notable attractions to check out:

2. **Kulturforum**: This complex houses several important cultural institutions, including the Museum of Musical Instruments and the New National Gallery. It’s a short walk from Berlin Modern and offers a broader exploration of art and culture.

3. **Potsdamer Platz**: This bustling square is a hub of entertainment and commerce, featuring shopping centers, restaurants, and cinemas. A stroll through this area provides insight into Berlin’s modern urban life.

4. **Tiergarten**: Just a short distance away, this expansive park offers a peaceful retreat from the city's hustle and bustle. Perfect for a leisurely walk or a picnic, it’s a great place to relax after exploring the museum.

5. **Berlin Wall Memorial**: A poignant reminder of the city’s divided past, this site offers historical insights into the impact of the Berlin Wall. It’s an important cultural landmark that complements the themes explored in modern art.
