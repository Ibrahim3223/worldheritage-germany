Nestled within the vibrant cultural landscape of Stuttgart, this museum offers visitors an intriguing glimpse into the political and social history of post-war Germany. Theodor-Heuss-Haus was established to honor Heuss's memory and to convey the principles he stood for, including democracy, tolerance, and social responsibility.
