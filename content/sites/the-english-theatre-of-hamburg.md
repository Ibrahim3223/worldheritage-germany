The English Theatre of Hamburg is conveniently located near several noteworthy attractions that can complement your visit:

1. **Hamburg Kunsthalle**: Just a short distance away, this impressive art museum showcases a vast collection of European art, ranging from the Middle Ages to contemporary works.
   
2. **Speicherstadt**: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this historic warehouse district offers picturesque canals and stunning architecture, making it a great place to explore before or after your theater experience.

3. **Miniatur Wunderland**: One of the largest model railway exhibitions in the world, this attraction is perfect for families and offers a unique perspective on various landscapes and cultures.

4. **Elbphilharmonie**: An architectural masterpiece and concert hall located near the harbor, the Elbphilharmonie hosts a variety of musical performances and is an iconic part of Hamburg’s skyline.

5. **St. Michael's Church**: Known locally as Michel, this church is a landmark of Hamburg and offers stunning views from its tower, along with beautiful baroque architecture to admire.

These attractions provide a well-rounded experience of Hamburg’s cultural offerings, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in art, history, and music.
