The mounds can reach considerable heights, and their shapes are often characterized by a noticeable bulge at the center, which is where the burial itself would have taken place. The construction of these mounds required significant effort and coordination, as they were often built over the remains of the deceased along with grave goods that may have included pottery, tools, and personal items.
