Ebertswiese is situated within a region rich in natural beauty and cultural heritage. Nearby attractions that visitors may find interesting include:

3. **Grimm's Fairy Tale Route**: This scenic route celebrates the stories of the Brothers Grimm and features numerous castles and villages that inspired their tales.

5. **Eisenach**: A charming town that offers cultural attractions, including museums and theaters, as well as historical sites related to Martin Luther.
