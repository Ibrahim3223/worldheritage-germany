As you approach this historic site, the atmosphere is steeped in centuries of faith and community, inviting visitors to reflect on the spiritual journeys that have unfolded within its walls. The surrounding landscape harmonizes with the church's ancient stones, providing a serene backdrop that contrasts with the vibrant life of modern Düsseldorf.
