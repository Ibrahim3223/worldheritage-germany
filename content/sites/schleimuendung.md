The reserve is significant for its ecological diversity, serving as a vital habitat for various bird species, marine life, and other wildlife. The area is not only a sanctuary for nature but also a place where visitors can engage with the natural world, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers, birdwatchers, and outdoor enthusiasts.
