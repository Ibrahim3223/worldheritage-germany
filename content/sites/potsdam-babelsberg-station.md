## Nearby Attractions

1. **Sanssouci Park**: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this expansive park is home to the magnificent Sanssouci Palace, the summer residence of Frederick the Great. The park features beautifully landscaped gardens and various other historical buildings.

2. **New Palace (Neues Palais)**: Located within Sanssouci Park, this Baroque palace is known for its opulent architecture and lavish interiors. It was built in the 18th century and reflects the grandeur of the Prussian monarchy.

3. **Filmpark Babelsberg**: Just a short distance from the station, this film studio and theme park offers insights into the world of cinema through various attractions, shows, and exhibitions.

4. **Babelsberg Castle**: Situated along the banks of the Havel River, this castle is a picturesque example of Romantic architecture and is surrounded by beautiful parkland.

5. **Potsdam Museum**: For those interested in the local history and culture, this museum provides exhibitions that delve into the rich heritage of Potsdam and its development over the centuries.
