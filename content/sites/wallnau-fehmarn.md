This protected area is a haven for migratory birds and various wildlife, making it a critical site for conservation and ecological research. Spanning a range of habitats from wetlands to heathlands, Wallnau offers a unique opportunity for nature enthusiasts, bird watchers, and photographers to experience the natural beauty of northern Germany. Whether you are seeking tranquility in nature or an educational experience about local flora and fauna, this reserve promises a fulfilling visit.

Wallnau’s importance extends beyond its natural beauty; it plays a vital role in the preservation of wildlife and offers insights into the delicate balance of ecosystems.
