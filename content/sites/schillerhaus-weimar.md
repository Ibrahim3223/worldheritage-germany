2. **Bauhaus Museum Weimar**: Celebrating the influential Bauhaus school of art and design, this museum showcases the movement's history and its impact on modern architecture and design.

3. **Cranach House**: Known for its historical significance, this house was once home to the famous painter Lucas Cranach the Elder. It now serves as a museum that illustrates the life of Cranach and the art of the Renaissance period.

4. **Weimar Palace**: A stunning example of Baroque architecture, the palace offers guided tours that delve into its history and the royal family that once resided there. The surrounding park is also a lovely place for a stroll.
