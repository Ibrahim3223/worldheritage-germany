## Overview

The Museum Yard Göhren invites visitors to immerse themselves in the cultural heritage of the Mönchgut region on Rügen Island, in the northern part of Germany. Established in 1971, this open-air museum serves as a vivid reflection of the traditional rural life of the area, showcasing historical buildings and artifacts that narrate the story of the region's agricultural and maritime past. As you wander through the museum, you will encounter structures that have been meticulously preserved and relocated to create a cohesive experience that illustrates local customs and the evolution of everyday life. 

Situated in the picturesque backdrop of Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, the museum stands as a portal to the region's history, inviting both locals and tourists to engage with the rich traditions of the past. The Museum Yard Göhren is an exceptional destination for those interested in experiencing the authentic lifestyle of earlier generations while exploring the scenic beauty of Rügen.
