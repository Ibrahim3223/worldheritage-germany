## Overview

The site serves not only as a historical landmark but also as a cultural hub, where art and commerce intersect. With its unique architecture and rich history, Gebauer Höfe invites exploration and appreciation, making it a noteworthy stop for those interested in Berlin's architectural and industrial narrative.

As Berlin transformed throughout the decades, so too did Gebauer Höfe.
