The Paradiesgarten is conveniently located near several attractions that enhance the overall experience of visiting Potsdam. Here are a few notable places to consider exploring:

1. **Sanssouci Palace**: Just a short distance from the Paradiesgarten, Sanssouci Palace is one of the most iconic landmarks in Potsdam. This summer palace of Frederick the Great is renowned for its stunning architecture and beautifully landscaped gardens.

2. **Neues Palais**: Another remarkable palace nearby, the Neues Palais features opulent interiors and expansive grounds. The palace serves as a testament to the grandeur of Prussian royalty and is worth a visit for its historical significance and artistic treasures.

3. **Orangery Palace**: Located within the Sanssouci Park, the Orangery Palace is a stunning example of late Baroque architecture. Its gardens and architecture provide a picturesque setting for a leisurely stroll.

4. **Cecilienhof Palace**: This palace is famous for hosting the Potsdam Conference in 1945. Today, it stands as a museum that delves into its historical significance, offering insights into post-war Europe.
