Initially developed as a market square, it has served various functions throughout its existence, adapting to the changing needs of the community. This connection to Esperanto resonates with the district's diverse population, embodying the spirit of inclusivity and cultural exchange.

The significance of Esperantoplatz extends beyond its historical roots; it is a gathering place for cultural events, markets, and community activities.
