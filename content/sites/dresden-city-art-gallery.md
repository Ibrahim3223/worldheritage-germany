## Nearby Attractions

After your visit to the Dresden City Art Gallery, consider exploring these nearby attractions that complement your artistic journey:

1. **Zwinger Palace**: This historic site is a magnificent example of Baroque architecture and houses several museums, including the Old Masters Picture Gallery. The palace grounds are beautifully landscaped, perfect for a leisurely stroll.

2. **Dresden Frauenkirche**: A symbol of resilience, this stunning church was reconstructed after its destruction during World War II. Its impressive dome and detailed interior make it well worth a visit.

3. **Semper Opera House**: Renowned for its stunning architecture and exceptional acoustics, the opera house is an iconic cultural venue in Dresden. Consider catching a performance if your schedule allows.

4. **Brühl's Terrace**: Known as the "Balcony of Europe," this scenic promenade offers lovely views of the Elbe River and the cityscape. It's an ideal spot to relax after a day of exploring.

5. **Dresden Castle**: One of the oldest buildings in the city, this castle features a fascinating mix of architectural styles and serves as the home to several museums, including the Historic Green Vault.
