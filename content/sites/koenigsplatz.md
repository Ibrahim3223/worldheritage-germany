## Nearby Attractions

Königsplatz is surrounded by several notable attractions that enhance the experience of visiting the square:

1. **Lenbachhaus**: This renowned art museum is located adjacent to Königsplatz and houses an extensive collection of works from the Blue Rider movement and other significant artists.

2. **The Glyptothek**: A short walk from the square, this museum features an impressive collection of Greek and Roman sculptures, providing insight into ancient art and culture.

3. **The State Museum of Egyptian Art**: Located nearby, this museum showcases an extensive array of artifacts from ancient Egypt, making it a fascinating stop for history enthusiasts.
