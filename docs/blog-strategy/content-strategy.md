# WorldHeritage.Guide Blog Content Strategy

## Overview

This document defines the content strategy for the WorldHeritage.Guide blog, establishing content pillars, target audiences, and strategic goals.

## Target Audience

**Primary Audiences:**
1. **Heritage Travelers** (35-60 years old)
   - High disposable income
   - Value authentic experiences over mass tourism
   - Research extensively before travel
   - Willing to go off beaten path

2. **Culture Enthusiasts** (25-55 years old)
   - College-educated
   - Interest in history, architecture, art
   - Read extensively about destinations
   - Share content on social media

3. **History Buffs** (40-70 years old)
   - Deep interest in European history
   - Academic or professional background
   - Seek detailed, accurate information
   - Value expert perspectives

4. **Photography Enthusiasts** (25-50 years old)
   - Amateur to professional photographers
   - Chase best light and compositions
   - Active on Instagram/photography communities
   - Value technical advice

**Secondary Audiences:**
- Students (research for papers)
- Teachers (educational resources)
- Travel bloggers (reference material)
- Architecture professionals

## Strategic Goals

**Traffic Goals:**
- Month 1-3: 5,000 monthly visitors
- Month 4-6: 15,000 monthly visitors
- Month 7-12: 50,000 monthly visitors
- Year 2: 150,000+ monthly visitors

**Engagement Goals:**
- Average time on page: 4+ minutes
- Bounce rate: Under 60%
- Pages per session: 3+
- Click-through to heritage sites: 15%+

**SEO Goals:**
- Rank in top 10 for 50+ keywords (6 months)
- Earn 100+ backlinks from quality domains (12 months)
- Domain authority increase to 40+ (12 months)

**Conversion Goals:**
- 20% of blog readers explore heritage site pages
- 10% use trip planner (when launched)
- 5% subscribe to newsletter (when launched)

## Content Mix Strategy

**Ideal Monthly Mix (8-10 posts):**
- 3 Travel Guides
- 2 Cultural Heritage
- 2 History
- 2 Photography
- 1-2 Travel Tips
- 1 Conservation

**Content Type Distribution:**
- 30% Ultimate Guides (comprehensive, 3000-5000 words)
- 25% Listicles (numbered lists, 1500-2500 words)
- 20% How-To Guides (practical, step-by-step, 1800-2500 words)
- 10% Comparison Posts (side-by-side, 2000-3000 words)
- 10% Photography Tutorials (technical, 1500-2500 words)
- 5% Historical Narratives (story-driven, 2000-3000 words)

- Dr. Sophia Weber: 30% (heritage expert perspective)
- Max Fischer: 40% (practical travel focus)

## Content Philosophy

**Quality Over Quantity:**
- Better to publish 2 excellent posts per week than 5 mediocre ones
- Each post should be the definitive resource on its topic
- Aim to be the #1 result for target keyword

**Evergreen Focus:**
- 80% evergreen content (remains relevant for years)
- 20% timely content (events, seasons, news)
- Update evergreen content annually

**User Intent First:**
- Write for humans, not search engines
- Answer the question they're really asking
- Provide value beyond what they expected

**Authority Building:**
- Cite sources (academic, official)
- Use author expertise strategically
- Demonstrate firsthand knowledge
