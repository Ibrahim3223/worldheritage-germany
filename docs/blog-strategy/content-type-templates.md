# Blog Content Type Templates

This document provides detailed templates for each of the 8 core content types used on WorldHeritage.Guide blog.

---

## Template 1: ULTIMATE GUIDE

**Purpose:** Create the definitive, comprehensive resource on a topic
**Target Length:** 3000-5000 words
**Typical Author:** Dr. Sophia Weber, Prof. Martin Schmidt
**Publishing Frequency:** 1-2 per month
**SEO Value:** Very High (targets high-volume keywords)

### Title Formulas

- "The Ultimate Guide to [Topic] in Germany"
- "Complete Guide to [Region]'s Heritage Sites"
- "Everything You Need to Know About [Topic]"
- "[Topic]: The Definitive Guide"
- "Mastering [Topic]: Complete Resource"

### Structure Template

```markdown
---
title: "[Title with keyword]"
description: "[150-160 chars with keyword and value proposition]"
date: [Date]
author: [Author slug]
blog_category: "[Category]"
tags: ["[Tag1]", "[Tag2]", "[Tag3]", "[Tag4]", "[Tag5]"]
featured_image: "/images/[image-path]"
excerpt: "[Compelling 2-3 sentence excerpt]"
featured: false
toc: true
---
```

### Key Elements

**Must Include:**
- Minimum 15 internal links (mix of heritage sites and blog posts)
- 8-15 H2 sections with H3 subsections
- Table of contents (enabled with `toc: true`)
- Multiple images (minimum 1 per major section)
- Comparison table (if applicable)
- "Pro Tip" callouts throughout
- FAQ section (8-12 questions)
- Author bio at end

**Image Requirements:**
- Featured image: High-impact, 1920x1080px minimum
- Section images: 1 per major section
- Comparison tables or infographics (when relevant)
- All images: Optimized WebP, descriptive alt text

**Internal Linking Strategy:**
- Link to 10-15 relevant heritage site pages
- Link to 3-5 related blog posts
- Use contextual anchor text (not "click here")
- Distribute links throughout (not all at end)

## Template 2: LISTICLE

**Purpose:** Engaging, scannable list format that performs well socially
**Target Length:** 1500-2500 words
**Typical Author:** Max Fischer, Anna Hoffmann
**Publishing Frequency:** 2-3 per month
**SEO Value:** High (targets "best [topic]" keywords)

### Title Formulas

- "[Number] Best [Category] in Germany"
- "[Number] Hidden [Category] You Must Visit"
- "[Number] Most [Adjective] Heritage Sites in [Region]"
- "Top [Number] [Category] for [Audience]"
- "[Number] [Category] That Will [Emotion]"

**Number Selection:**
- Use odd numbers (feels more authentic)
- 10-15 items: Quick, digestible list
- 20-25 items: Comprehensive roundup
- 50+: Ultimate, link-magnet list
