## Publishing Schedule

**Regular Schedule:**
- **Monday:** 9:00 AM CET (major post of week)
- **Thursday:** 9:00 AM CET (supporting post)
- **Optional Wednesday/Friday:** Scale to 3-4 posts/week

**Why These Days:**
- Monday: Fresh start, high engagement
- Thursday: Mid-week traffic boost
- Avoid weekends: Lower engagement

**Time Zone Considerations:**
- 9:00 AM CET = 3:00 AM EST (catches US morning)
- Also good for European readers

**Analyze:**
- Which posts performed best?
- Which topics drove most traffic?
- What keyword opportunities emerged?

- What content types work best?

**Adjust:**
